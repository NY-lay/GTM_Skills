# Plumcot Business Context

## Company Overview
Plumcot is a GenAI Value Realization Platform that transforms AI projects from "demo cool" to "business useful" through human-AI collaboration.

## Product Lines

### 1. Core Platform - GenAI Value Realization
**Target**: Enterprise organizations deploying GenAI applications
**Problem**: 90% of GenAI projects fail to deliver business value due to poor reliability
**Solution**: Platform bringing developers, users, and executives together to drive accuracy, adoption, and measurable ROI

**Key Features**:
- Smart feedback collection from users on AI outputs
- Continuous improvement loops with reinforcement learning
- Expert-in-the-loop escalation system
- ROI and business impact tracking
- Executive dashboards for portfolio management

**Pricing**:
- Starter: Free (up to 3 GenAI apps)
- Growth: $99/month per GenAI app
- Enterprise: Contact sales

### 2. SaaS AI Impact Measurement
**Target**: SaaS companies with AI-powered products competing on feature differentiation
**Problem**: AI features becoming commoditized, hard to prove value
**Solution**: Neutral "AI Impact Score" providing objective proof of business value

**Pricing**: $10K setup + $30/customer/month

### 3. Insurance Brokerage AI Chief of Staff
**Target**: Commercial insurance brokerages ($1-3M revenue, 15-person teams)
**Problem**: Drowning in email, contract review, deal follow-ups
**Solution**: AI assistant handling contract review, COI verification, email/calendar management

**Key Proof Points**:
- 4-hour contract reviews → 5 minutes
- Catches coverage contradictions manual review misses
- 10+ hours saved per week

**Pricing**: $500/month (requires separate Claude subscription)

## ICPs by Vertical

### Enterprise Platform ICP
- **Title**: VP/Director of AI/ML, Chief Data Officer, VP Engineering
- **Company**: Fortune 500, Financial Services, Healthcare, Technology
- **Stage**: Has GenAI projects at POC struggling to reach production
- **Pain**: Poor reliability, unclear ROI, no governance framework

### SaaS ICP
- **Title**: VP Product, Head of AI/ML, CEO (for smaller companies)
- **Company**: B2B SaaS with AI features
- **Pain**: AI features becoming table stakes, hard to differentiate, customers questioning value

### Insurance ICP
- **Title**: Brokerage Owner, Operations Manager
- **Company**: Commercial insurance brokerage, 10-30 employees
- **Verticals**: Construction, Real Estate, Transportation
- **Pain**: Manual contract review, email overload, missed follow-ups

## Key Differentiators

**vs. Analytics tools (Mixpanel/Pendo)**: Tracks business impact, not just engagement
**vs. Model monitoring (Arize)**: Measures customer outcomes, not just technical metrics
**vs. CS tools (Gainsight)**: Specifically measures AI impact on retention/expansion
**vs. Building internally**: Provides neutral third-party validation
**vs. ChatGPT**: Domain-trained AI that understands specific business logic
